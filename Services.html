Media Strategy

Austine's media strategy has been instrumental in driving the success of Trend 100.9 FM, positioning it as the number 1 radio station in the region. With his remarkable career in the media as a news reporter for two national television stations in Nigeria, MBI and NTA, Austine has demonstrated his leadership skills and ability to achieve results in a highly competitive media landscape. As the Head of Marketing and Strategy at Trend 100.9 FM, Austine has made significant contributions to the station's success. He has been responsible for leading and executing all marketing, communication, and advertising strategies, resulting in increased revenue generation and brand recognition. One of Austine's key strengths is his strategic organizational partnerships. He has forged strategic alliances with relevant stakeholders in the media industry, including advertisers, sponsors, and other media outlets, to expand Trend 100.9 FM's reach and influence. These partnerships have not only increased the station's revenue but also provided unique opportunities for cross-promotion and collaboration, further elevating the station's profile in the media landscape. In addition, Austine has shown his innovative approach to overhauling radio shows to be commercially viable. He has introduced creative concepts, engaging content, and interactive segments that resonate with the target audience, attracting more listeners and advertisers. Austine's ability to identify and tap into the pulse of the audience has been a key factor in Trend 100.9 FM's success in gaining and retaining a loyal listener base.
