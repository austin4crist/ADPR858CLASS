About Me

Austine is a highly accomplished transformative leader, TEDx Organizer, Maxwell-certified public speaker, Mandela Washington fellow, radio host, and non-profit founder with a strong passion for building systems and empowering individuals to achieve their fullest potential. With a wealth of experience and expertise in various fields, Austine has earned a reputation for his exceptional leadership skills and ability to drive results.

With a remarkable career in the media as a news reporter for two national television stations in Nigeria, MBI and NTA, Austine joined Trend 100.9 FM and became a prominent voice and radio host, demonstrating his leadership skills and ability to achieve results in a competitive media landscape

Austine made significant contributions to the success of Trend 100.9FM as the Head of Marketing and Strategy. With an impressive track record of leading and executing all marketing, communication, and advertising strategies for the station in Nigeria, Austine was instrumental in driving increased revenue generation and propelling Trend 100.9FM to become the number 1 radio station in the entire region. Austine's strategic organizational partnerships and innovative overhaul of radio shows to be commercially viable have been key to the station's success

A Maxwell-certified public speaker, Austine has a unique talent for captivating audiences with his compelling messages and impactful presentations. He is renowned for his ability to set people up for success by providing them with exposure, connections, and resources to pursue their dreams and become better individuals.

Austine's commitment to leadership and excellence is evident in his recognition as a Mandela Washington fellow, a prestigious program by the U.S. Department of State that acknowledges emerging African leaders. He has also made a significant impact as a radio host, where his prominent voice has been heard for years, showcasing his exceptional communication skills and ability to connect with audiences.

In addition to his professional achievements, Austine is the founder of Ideals and Realities Youth Empowerment Foundation (IRYEF), a non-profit organization that invests in the potential of individuals and makes a positive impact on communities. Through IRYEF, Austine has demonstrated his unwavering commitment to social impact and empowering youth to become change-makers in their communities. He is the curator of the annual TEDxOkpanam.

Austine has received multiple awards for his outstanding contributions. He received two consecutive awards as Staff of the Year and Marketer of the Year at Trend 100.9FM in 2021, showcasing his exceptional performance and commitment to excellence. Austine has also been honoured with the Delta Unsung Heroes Award for his social impact and the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) Honors Award in recognition of his selfless and patriotic service to Nigeria, among numerous other accolades.
